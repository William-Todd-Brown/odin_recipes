Dragon's breath chili

Chili so hot it will make a dragon cry

Eat this to keep warm during the winter.

    Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons of bacon grease or oil
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 Anaheim chilies, roasted, peeled, chopped
  • 3 poblano chilies, roasted, peeled, chopped
  • 2 red bell peppers, diced
  • 2 jalapenos chilies, minced
  • 2 yellow onions, diced
  • 1 head of garlic, minced
  • 1 pound boneless chuck, trimmed, and cut into 1/4-inch cubes
  • 2 pounds ground beef, coarse grind
  • 1 pound bulk italian sausage
  • 3 tablespoons chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons granulated garlic
  • 2 teaspoons granulated onion
  • 2 teaspoons hot paprika
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 2 teaspoons freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 cup tomato paste
  • 12oz large beer
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 2 15.5oz cans kidney beans, with juice
  • 2 15.5oz cans pinto beans, with juice
  • 1 bunch green onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar
  • 1 cup coffee

    Steps

  1. cook the peppers and meat
  2. add the tomato sauce and paste
  3. Serve chili in bowls
