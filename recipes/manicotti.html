Manicotti

The second best manicotti you will eat

This recipe show how to make a staple of delicious food

    Ingredients

  • 8oz Manicotti shells
  • 24oz Whole milk Ricotta
  • 1 cup Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 4oz Cream cheese
  • 1/2 Cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 Large egg
  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • 3 tablespoons finely chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon table salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 cups Marinara sauce

    Steps

  1. Boil your pasta
  2. Make the filling
  3. Fill each manicotti
  4. Cover and bake
